Resume Manager is an online resume editor and repository provided by UP CAPES for the students of College of Engineering in University of the Philippines Diliman.

Tools

Codeigniter

Create a resume

Students are provided with a web interface where they can input details required to generate their resumes. They can also choose to include their own resume file.
